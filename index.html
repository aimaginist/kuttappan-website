✦ The Story

A Village. A Boy.
Infinite Plans.

In the global landscape of children's animation, there are hundreds of shows for children everywhere — but almost nothing for a child growing up in Kerala. Kuttappan changes that.

Set in the fictional village of Thottumkara on a backwater canal, Kuttappan follows an 8-year-old boy whose plans are magnificent, whose failures are spectacular, and whose heart is the biggest thing in the village. Think Pixar's visual ambition meeting an Ammachi's kitchen — warm, funny, and unmistakably Kerala.

✦ The Cast

Meet the People
of Thottumkara

⭐ HERO
Kuttappan
കുട്ടപ്പൻ The Planner · The Dreamer · 8 years old

Overconfident in his plans. Magnificent in his disasters. Kuttappan sees every problem as an adventure waiting to happen — and every failure as a better story than success. Cream shirt, curly afro, always barefoot.

"The plan is simple. All we need is the goat, Shambhu's umbrella, and exactly fourteen coconut shells."
🏠 MORAL CENTER
Ammachi
അമ്മച്ചി The Grandmother · The Wise One · Always Right

Compact, formidable, and the funniest person in Thottumkara. She doesn't raise her voice — she lowers it. She has a proverb for every disaster. And she always knew it was coming.

"I am not saying the plan is bad. I am saying that the last time you made this face, we had to apologize to four neighbors and one rooster."
💚 BEST FRIEND
Shambhu
ശംഭു The Heart · The Conscience · Always Loyal

He sees every disaster coming. He goes along anyway. Shambhu is the most emotionally intelligent boy in Thottumkara — green t-shirt, red thread bracelet, messy hair, and an endless, loyal heart.

"I'm with you. I'm always with you. I just want to note, for the record, that I said this."
😏 FRENEMY
Pakru
പക്രു The Rival · The Snacker · Secretly Wants In

The richest kid in Thottumkara. Always eating. His family has a color television AND a scooter. He pretends not to want to join Kuttappan's plans. He always ends up in them.

"I could join you. If you want. I don't want to. I'm just saying I could."

✦ The World

Welcome to
Thottumkara

തൊട്ടുംകര

A fictional village on a backwater canal in central Kerala. Population: about 400 families. Adventures: unlimited. The world as vivid, funny, and emotionally generous as any fictional universe in the history of animation.

🏠
Ammachi's House
വീട്

The laterite-stone house where every plan is hatched on the thinnai steps. The kitchen is Ammachi's territory — absolutely.

🛶
The Backwater Canal
കായൽ

Luminously teal at morning. Dark and mysterious at dusk. Always beautiful. The spine of Thottumkara and the call of the world beyond.

🏫
Primary School
പഠശാല

Where Krishnan Master's magnificent moustache and Kuttappan's magnificent plans have their daily confrontation.

🥭
Mango Grove
മാവിന്തോപ്പ്

Seven trees. Disputed ownership since before Kuttappan's father was born. The best mangoes in the district. Kuttappan's strategy room.

🪔
Temple Courtyard
അമ്പലപ്പറമ്പ്

Where festivals happen, village news travels faster than the wind, and the sacred heartbeat of Thottumkara pulses loudest.

🌾
Paddy Fields
നെൽപ്പാടം

Floods in monsoon and turns to a luminous sky-reflecting lake. Golden in harvest. The deep, ancient rhythm of Thottumkara.
